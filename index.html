About Me

Hi! This is Pavan Kumar Paluri.
I am a final-year Computer Science PhD Candidate at the University of Houston.
A Research Assistant at Real Time Systems Laboratory @ UH. Dr. Albert Cheng is my research advisor.
My areas of interest include Real Time Systems, CPU-Virtualization, Operating Systems, Linux Kernel Programming, Xen Schedulers, Space-Mission-Critical Systems
